## 1. Problem Statement

Completing a puzzle is the primary reward moment, but the win overlay appears abruptly. A gentle celebration animation — a soft scale/fade on the overlay and optional subtle board emphasis — makes completion feel satisfying while staying calm. Like placement animations, win motion must respect Reduce Motion.

## 2. Goals

- Animate the win overlay appearance with a gentle transition.
- Optionally add a subtle, non-distracting board completion emphasis (e.g. soft pulse on placed animals).
- Respect Reduce Motion (instant overlay when enabled).
- Coordinate with win SFX (P4.5) without delaying the overlay.

## 3. Non-Goals

- Confetti, fireworks, or high-energy effects.
- Placement/remove animations (P5.1).
- Changing win-screen content or stats (P3.15, P4.6).
- Haptics (P5.6).

## 4. Personas & User Stories

- **As a player**, I want a satisfying completion moment so that finishing feels rewarding.
- **As a motion-sensitive player**, I want the win screen to appear instantly when Reduce Motion is on.
- **As a developer**, I want win animation to reuse motion constants from P5.1.

## 5. Functional Requirements

- **FR-1.** When the puzzle completes, the win overlay MUST animate in gently (e.g. fade + slight scale).
- **FR-2.** When Reduce Motion is enabled, the overlay MUST appear immediately without scale/translate.
- **FR-3.** Win animation MUST NOT prevent the board from locking or block Play Again.
- **FR-4.** Win SFX (P4.5) MAY play concurrently; animation MUST NOT wait for audio.
- **FR-5.** Animation duration SHOULD reuse centralized motion constants from P5.1.

## 6. Non-Functional Requirements

- **Performance** — Overlay animation does not drop frames; board remains static after lock.
- **Accessibility** — Reduce Motion honored; overlay content remains VoiceOver-focusable during/after animation.
- **Privacy** — N/A.
- **Maintainability** — Win transition defined in one modifier on `WinScreen`.
- **Reliability** — Win animation fires exactly once per completion; replay/reset does not replay until next win.

## 7. Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1.** *Given* Reduce Motion off, *when* the puzzle completes, *then* the win overlay animates in within ~300ms.
- **AC-2.** *Given* Reduce Motion on, *when* the puzzle completes, *then* the overlay appears instantly.
- **AC-3.** *Given* the win overlay is shown, *when* Play Again is tapped, *then* the overlay dismisses and the board resets without animation glitches.
- **AC-4.** *Given* completion, *when* observed, *then* the win animation plays once (no loop).

## 8. Data Model

No new model. Triggered by `GameSession` completion flag / `GameViewModel` win state.

## 9. API Surface

N/A — internal SwiftUI transition on `WinScreen`.

## 10. UI / UX

- Win overlay: fade + slight scale from center (Reduce Motion off).
- Optional: very subtle opacity pulse on animal cells once (not required for AC).
- Dismiss: fade out on Play Again, or instant per Reduce Motion.
- States: hidden → animating in → visible → dismissed.
